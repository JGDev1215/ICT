Market Phases
Use these as context labels, not mechanical predictions. Start with the higher-timeframe draw and current dealing range.
Condition 1

Consolidation

Price is contained inside a range, often building liquidity on both sides.

Condition 2

Expansion

Price displaces away from a range, liquidity pool, or key level.

Condition 3

Retracement

Price returns into a prior dealing range, FVG, order block, or premium/discount area.

Condition 4

Reversal

Price changes delivery direction after a liquidity objective, failed continuation, or MSS.
