Simple rules
Keep the workflow clean

1. Instrument

Pick one instrument. Do not mix levels across unrelated markets.

2. HTF draw

Identify the draw on liquidity first. Above price usually points to buy-side draw; below price usually points to sell-side draw.

3. LTF sweep

Wait for the lower timeframe to sweep the opposing pool before treating the idea as focused.

4. Final save

Edit the saved card, save changes, then final save after selecting an outcome. Only final-saved Hit/Miss cards enter the hit-rate sample.
